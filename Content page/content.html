Actions and Progress

During the pandemic era, the progress towards achieving quality education was very slow because of the impact on learning. Still there have been a slight increase in primary and secondary school completion rates globally. Despite the efforts of UN targets, many schools don't aim to achieve these goals. UN works closely with governments, organizations and private sector to hold partnerships and collaborations with them.Which interms helps with better education systems and teaching systems. UN increased investment in education to address the problems rising for it.

The UN provides technical assistance and capacity-building support to countries, particularly in low-income regions, to strengthen their education systems, improve teacher qualifications, and enhance digital literacy. Training programs and workshops help build local capacity to address the unique challenges faced by each community. The UN has launched various global education initiatives aimed at promoting access to quality education for all, such as the Education 2030 Agenda and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), which include specific targets for achieving universal access to education, improving literacy rates, and enhancing the quality of education.
